# End of the Line Rail Company

Also known as the ELR Company, or simply the Company, the End of the Line
Rail Company is the oldest running rail company of Dragongirlserver. The
Company began with a single East-West line, mirrored in the Nether, which
had just two connections.

Now, along with its rail lines, the Company maintains the ELR Manual in
collaboration with all other rail-involved entities of the server, to
standardize train and track configurations ensuring interoperability
across the entire server.

This text provides further information on the Company, as well as a copy
of the ELR Manual in full. Read on for more.
